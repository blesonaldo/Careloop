Everything you need to know about Careloop

What is Careloop?+
Careloop is a customer relationship management tool designed to help business owners remember follow-ups, track customer activity, and maintain better relationships over time.
How does Careloop work?+
Careloop helps you add customers, track purchases, and receive smart follow-up suggestions based on customer activity. It keeps your follow-ups organized so you always know who to reconnect with next.
Will Careloop send messages automatically?+
No. Careloop focuses on intentional relationship management. It suggests the right time to follow up, while you stay in control of when and how messages are sent.
Can I track returning customers?+
Yes. Careloop allows you to record purchases and monitor customer activity so you can better manage repeat customers and long-term relationships.
Is Careloop only for large businesses?+
Not at all. Careloop is built primarily for business owners, freelancers, and growing teams who want a simple way to manage customer relationships without using a complicated CRM.
